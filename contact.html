bugs

Found a bug? I'll be grateful for any bugs or spelling mistakes reported and will do my best to fix them as soon as I am able to.

before contacting me

do:

  • Tell me which theme and version (if applicable).
  • Describe your problem: How does it present itself? What have you tried? What happened? If possible include a link.
  • Don't want a public answer? Let me know in your message!
  • Have patience and don't be rude.

don't:

  • Questions already covered by the FAQ are likely to be ignored.
  • Want to customise something? Try to figure it out yourself first. Google is your friend.
  • I can't help with major customisations but I can point you in the right direction.
  • I don't provide support for other people's themes.
